About TempleStorm

At TempleStorm, we proudly embrace our compact size, allowing us to confidently declare ourselves as the top contenders in our field.

Headquartered in the vibrant landscape of West Michigan, our dynamic team comprises three dedicated individuals hailing from Davenport University. While our backgrounds and expertise levels may vary, we are all passionately immersed in the realm of web development. With most of us set to graduate within the year, our collective drive and enthusiasm propel us towards excellence.

It's important to note that any design elements, be it logos, backgrounds, or images, are meticulously curated with educational intent. We collaborate with talented external contractors who generously offer their services pro bono, enriching our projects with their invaluable contributions. Should you have any inquiries about our endeavors, please feel free to reach out via email through our Contact Us page.
